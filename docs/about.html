About us

At Country Inn Cafe, we do our best to make you feel at home, the instant you walk in the door. We are a family owned restaurant that has maintained our roots in tasty homemade food for over years. Yes, you could say we are a local favorite.
Fresh ingredients are delivered daily and most dishes are made from scratch. The  most important thing for we is: the quality.
Breakfast is our specialty. You could say it made us famous. You can enjoy our famous breakfast up until closing. All day, everyday.
